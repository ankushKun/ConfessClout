WTH is ConfessClout?

Often on many Discord servers I have found a channel in which users were able to send confessions anonymously
(any sort of message that one would not like to say publicly...kinda)

That means without disclosing the identity of the person who sent that message.
All this was done through the use of bots, the user would DM the bot
and the bot would send the message in its specific channel on the server.

Sounds fun right?
In a server that I manage on Discord, we have our own custom confession bot,
and it's really nice seeing people sharing their thoughts (both good and bad).

Many people are unable to release their thoughts and vent somewhere when needed, and end up keeping things to themselves, which has a negative effect on them :(
